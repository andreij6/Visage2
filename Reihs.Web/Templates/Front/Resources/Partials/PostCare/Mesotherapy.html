MESOTHERAPY (LIPODISSOLVE) POST CARE:
What to do after leaving
Le Beau Med Spa in Frisco, TX

 

  • If experiencing discomfort, use only non-inflammatory oral medication (i.e. Tylenol).
  • Do not apply ice of anti-inflammatory topical agent to treated area.
  • Do not perform exercise, massage or strenuous activity for 48 hours.
  • Patient is to expect some burning and/or discomfort for 20 minutes after treatment. In rare instances, there may also be some lingering pain experienced for 4 hours (including point discomfort) after treatment. In such cases, the patient is advised to use appropriate medication and contact the physician at our med spa in Frisco, TX.
  • Patient should expect swelling over the treated area, especially in the treatment of cellulite, for 48 to 72 hours.
  • After treating the lower lid fat pad; patients are required to remain in at least a 60 degree vertical position for 20 minutes, and are also required to sleep on 3 pillows for 2 days. Note: swelling of the eyelids is to be expected for 48 hours. If pain in or around the eye develops, contact the physician immediately.
  • After chin and/or jowl treatment; patients should expect swelling for 48 hours. Sleep with a head and chin wrap (i.e. scarf)
  • REMEMBER: Limit ice packs or anti-inflammatory medications for swelling of the eyes or face!
