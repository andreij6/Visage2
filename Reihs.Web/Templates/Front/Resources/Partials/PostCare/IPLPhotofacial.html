IPL-PHOTOFACIAL TREATMENT POST CARE:
What to expect after laser skin care
at Le Beau in Frisco, TX

 

  • Immediately after treatment, there should be mild temporary redness at the treatment site, which may last up to 2 hours or longer. Soothe the redness or heat with cold compresses after the treatment. Continue to apply cold compresses until heat in the skin has resolved.
  • Sun spots and age spot WILL DARKEN BEFORE they begin to “pepper”. DO NOT pick/rub these spot; they will desquamate (lift and fall off) on their own.
  • Cleanse the area with mild soap and water. DO NOT scrub.
  • DO NOT have chemical peels performed on the treated area for 7-10 days.
  • Anticipate some dryness and slight sloughing. The application of facial lotion/moisturizer is recommended.
  • Continue skincare regimen as directed by estheticians at our Frisco, Texas location. Apply additional moisturizer as needed.
  • A sunscreen (factor 30 or higher) must be applied to treated area on a regular daily basis; regardless of the weather conditions.
  • Direct sunlight/tanning beds must be avoided before/during/after treatment.
  • Make-up can be worn immediately after treatment.
  • Microdermabrasion should be performed 7-10 days post IPL treatment to assist in the exfoliation process.
