Skin Resurfacing

If aging, acne, or too much time in the sun has left your face with blotches, scars, wrinkles, or lines, laser skin resurfacing may help your skin look younger and healthier. Laser skin resurfacing removes skin layer by layer with precision. The new skin cells that form during healing give the skin a tighter, younger looking surface. The procedure can be performed alone with in combination with injectable treatments to the face.

Fractora™

Fractura™ is the ultimate non-invasive skin renovation without surgery. Fractora achieves full-scale treatment depths with customizable fractional energy to improve superficial skin tone problems (photo damage) to deep textural concerns (rhytids and tightening).

Fraxel™

Fraxel™ laser treatment has been cleared by the FDA to safely, gently and effectively resurface your skin from the inside out. That means improved texture, more even tone and smoother skin—all with minimal risks, and without discomfort and prolonged downtime of other procedures.

Chemical Peels

Chemical peels are a maintenance and preventive treatment – that are available in a variety of concentrations and intensities (strengths and types of acid) providing different results for different skin conditions or aesthetic needs.
