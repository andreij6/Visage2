Botox®

Botox® is derived from botulinum toxin and reduces wrinkles and fine lines by temporarily relaxing the muscle. The most common area of treatment is frown lines between the eyes but usage in other areas is common. It may also be used to treat excessive underarm sweating.

Dysport™

Dysport™ is an alternative form of botulinum toxin that reduces wrinkles and fine lines by temporarily relaxing the muscle. The most common area of treatment is frown lines between the eyes but usage in other areas is common. It may also be used to treat excessive underarm sweating.

Juvéderm™

Juvéderm™ is an FDA-approved injectable filler (facial filler) that is used to soften deep folds and reduce wrinkles in the faces of patients.Its prime use is removing nasolabial folds, or “smile lines,” creases of skin which run from the corners of the nose to the corners of the mouth.

Restylane®

Restylane® is used to add volume and fullness to the skin to correct moderate to severe facial wrinkles and folds, such as the lines from your nose to the corners of your mouth (nasolabial folds).

Perlane®

Restylane® is a soft tissue filler that treat moderate to mild creases around the mouth, but is used off label for plumping eyebrows, smoothing under-eye hollows, enhancing the lips, defining the cupid's bow (the curve of the upper lip), enhancing volume and defining contours of the cheeks, chin and jawline. Some doctors also use Perlane to soften the appearance of scars and other depressed facial marks.

Voluma®

Voluma® is used to minimize or eliminate lines and wrinkles that appear with aging; helping the skin to restore its firmness and contours by adding volume giving an enhanced appearance.

Sculptra®

Voluma® helps correct shallow to deep facial wrinkles, and folds, that appear with aging by replacing lost collagen.
